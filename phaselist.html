New Moon Phase

There is a total of 9 days that straddle The New Moon Phase.

Dinner

Take 5-9 days (ideally consecutive days and central to the New Moon):

Lunar Bioforce Powders

1/2 - 3/4 tsp of Renewal powder.

1/4 tsp of Maintenance powder.

General Information Re Powders:

  1. Can be taken between Dinner and Late Dinner. It may be swallowed with good food, fruit juice, herb tea, coffee (not instant coffee), red or white wines and purified water.
  2. A good option can be to mix Yogurt, Buttermilk or Kefir with high quality oil, and may be taken at any time.

Note: Use carbonated water only if you are taking a High Altitude, Long Distant Flight.

Breakfast

Optional—take 5-9 days (ideally consecutive days and central to the New Moon):

Lunar Bioforce Powders

1/2 - 3/4 tsp of Renewal powder.

1/4 tsp of Maintenance powder.
Pre Breakfast /
Post Dinner

Take on All Days

Take 8 drops of Animal Kingdom + 8 drops of Plant Kingdom = 16 drops of ELEMENTAL Tinctures. The total amount is 32 drops daily.

General Information Re Tinctures:

  1. The tinctures are to be mixed with natural juice & non-carbonated water, according to personal taste preference. It should not be less than ±250 ml. and that the ratio is ±20% of natural juice to ±80% of water.
  2. It is optional to take extra doses of PK&K Tinctures: i.e. Mid-Morning & Mid-Afternoon. An additional dose may be taken after fitness training… during hot/torrid weather… or if extra doses are needed to assist with temporary stress, whether emotional, philosophical or physical.
  3. If you prefer to take less of the PK&AK Tinctures, that is fine. It is your choice, let your taste dictate.

Note: Those who experience high blood sugar levels should use their discretion regarding the ratio of juice to water—or if a person is diagnosed by a Doctor as diabetic.
